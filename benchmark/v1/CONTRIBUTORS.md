## How to contribute items

See [CONTRIBUTING.md](../../CONTRIBUTING.md). New items are credited here on merge.

Authoring an item requires:

- Domain expertise in upstream / downstream / safety operations
- Native or near-native Arabic for items in Arabic
- Awareness that benchmark items become public CC-BY-4.0 once merged

Items contributed before formal review are kept in `benchmark/v1/proposed/` until the maintainer signs off.
